POST ETHANOL EVAP FROM OIL

ITEMS NEEDED:
~650mL Water
(FOUR) 4 liq-oz pH Up or other strong base

Heat base and enough water to cover residue to 70C. Stir vigorously at temperature for 3hrs. Rinse with water until glass is squeaky.
